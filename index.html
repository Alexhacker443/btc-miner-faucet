Payments


Payments will be made at .02 XMR until custom payment thresholds have been set. The first round of payments will go out on October 26th, 2017 if the site hits 0.5XMR. If the site does not reach the minimum payout limit by October 26th the site will pay out whenever the limit is hit. This is because the model that this site is based on that the miners can not be paid until the site is paid. Users will be payed in full for each hash they complete. This all depends upon the popularity of this site so please check back regularly for updates!
